Who we are

A married writing team that met at a science museum

Patrick Seim and Sam Seim are a married writing team who met, fittingly, on a first date at a science museum — and have been comparing notes ever since. A shared love of the sciences and of reading became the through-line of their life together.

Patrick is originally from Lincoln, Nebraska. Sam comes from the East Coast by way of West Virginia, and writes regularly on Substack at samseim.substack.com. They remain, by their own happy admission, the sort of people who still get a little too excited about a good museum exhibit and a better book.

What we write

Literary-leaning speculative fiction

Generation-ship science fiction, quiet supernatural horror, and dystopian SF. Books and words are the product — the machinery just runs underneath.
