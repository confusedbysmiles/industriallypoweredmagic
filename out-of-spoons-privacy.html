Out of Spoons

Privacy Policy

App: Out of Spoons (“the App”)
Developer: Industrially Powered Magic (“we,” “us,” “our”)
Effective date: July 6, 2026
Contact: support@industriallypoweredmagic.com

The short version

Out of Spoons is built on one simple promise: your data is yours.

  • Everything stays on your device. All the information you enter — your pain entries, body locations, pain types, triggers, notes, and assessments — is stored locally on your iPhone. It does not leave your device.
  • We never collect it. We have no servers, no accounts, and no way to see your data. We cannot read it, and we never receive it.
  • We never sell or share it. Your information is never sold, rented, traded, or shared with anyone, and it is never used for advertising.

The rest of this policy explains the details.

1. Information stored in the App

When you use Out of Spoons, you can record information such as:

  • Pain entries (severity, date and time)
  • Body locations where you feel pain
  • Pain types and triggers (including any custom ones you add)
  • Optional notes you write
  • Graded Chronic Pain Scale (GCPS-R) check-in responses and results

All of this information is stored locally on your device using Apple’s on-device storage. We do not have access to it.

2. We do not collect personal data

The App has no user accounts, no login, and no analytics or tracking tools. We do not operate servers that receive your information. We never collect, see, or store any of your personal or health information. By default, your data stays on your device. If you turn on iCloud sync (off by default — see “iCloud sync” below), your data is stored in your own private iCloud account so it can appear on your other devices; even then, it is never sent to us.

3. Health data (Apple HealthKit)

If you choose to connect the App to Apple Health, the App can read certain health information from HealthKit — such as sleep, activity, mindful minutes, or cycle data — solely to display it alongside your pain history on your device, to help you spot patterns. You control this access and can turn it off at any time in the iOS Health and Settings apps.

We follow Apple’s rules for health data:

  • Health data accessed through HealthKit is used only to provide features inside the App, on your device.
  • Health data is never transmitted off your device, sold, or shared with third parties.
  • Health data is never used for advertising or any data-mining purpose.
  • Health data is never stored in iCloud or any cloud service by the App.

iCloud sync (optional, off by default)

The App offers an optional iCloud sync setting so your pain data can appear on your other devices. It is off by default and only takes effect if you turn it on in Settings.

  • When on, your pain data is stored in your own private iCloud database under your Apple ID, using Apple’s CloudKit. It is governed by Apple’s iCloud terms and privacy policy.
  • We never receive, see, or store this data. There is no server we operate; sync happens directly between your devices and your private iCloud account.
  • Turning the setting off keeps your data only on the current device.
  • This is separate from HealthKit (Section 3): data the App reads from Apple Health is used only on-device and is never written to iCloud by the App.

4. We never sell or share your data

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your information with anyone. There are no advertisers, data brokers, or third-party services involved in the App.

5. When you choose to export or share

The App lets you export your own data (for example, as a CSV file) using the standard iOS share sheet. If you do this, you decide where that file goes — for example, emailing it to your doctor or saving it to Files. Once you share a file yourself, it is outside the App and this policy no longer controls it. We are not involved in and have no visibility into what you choose to export or share.

6. Data storage, retention, and deletion

Your data stays on your device for as long as you keep it. You can delete individual entries within the App at any time. Deleting the App from your device permanently removes all data stored by the App. Because we never receive your data, there is nothing for us to delete on our end.

7. Security

Your information is protected by your device’s own security, including your passcode, Face ID or Touch ID, and Apple’s device encryption. Keeping your device and iOS up to date helps keep your information safe.

8. Children’s privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children. Because all data stays on the device and is never collected by us, no personal information from any user — including children — is ever transmitted to or stored by us.

9. Medical disclaimer

Out of Spoons is a personal self-tracking tool. It is not a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any condition, and it does not provide medical advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional about your health and any treatment decisions.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above and post the revised policy at https://industriallypoweredmagic.com/out-of-spoons-privacy.html. Your continued use of the App after changes take effect means you accept the updated policy.

11. Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@industriallypoweredmagic.com.
