A trilogy — coming soon

The Consent Engine

A forthcoming dystopian science fiction trilogy by Sam & Patrick Seim. Book One is in progress.

Somewhere a machine keeps a ledger of every yes ever given, and calls it order. The people who feed it were promised that agreement, once recorded, could never be taken back — and slowly they are learning what it costs to have meant it. This is a story about the fine print of freedom, and the quiet, dangerous act of changing your mind.
